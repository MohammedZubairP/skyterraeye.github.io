Top land & engineering surveying companies in the UAE

A neutral overview of the market and a checklist to select the right survey partner for your scope.

Land vs engineering survey

In the UAE market, engineering survey typically refers to construction layout, as-built verification, leveling, and control work for projects. Land/cadastral work relates to official property boundaries and registration requirements. Always match your supplier to the exact scope and approvals needed.

How to shortlist a surveying company

  • Scope match: total station layout, as-built, topo, control
  • Deliverables: DWG layers + PDF report + coordinate tables
  • Site coordination: communication with engineers and consultants
  • QA/QC: documented checks and closure verification
  • Turnaround: realistic timelines and mobilization
