Platform

Everything between the drone flight and the client's decision

One platform covering detection, classification, plant hierarchy, field workflow and deliverables — for solar thermography and land-survey topography alike.

⚡

Automatic defect detection

Local-adaptive thermal analysis compares every pixel to its surrounding PV area — robust across temperature gradients and multi-block sites. Shape analysis classifies cells, diode strips, modules and strings automatically.

🏷

IEC 62446-3 classification

Technology-specific defect libraries with Class of Abnormality, typical ΔT ranges, failure causes and recommended actions — severity assigned automatically from ΔT.

▦

Module-level localisation

Draw block boundaries, define string × module grids, and every anomaly receives its plant-hierarchy ID (Block · String · Module) automatically — including future detections.

📊

Dashboards & analytics

Site health grade, severity trends across scan dates, block breakdowns, and indicative power & revenue-loss estimates driven by your tariff and module rating.

👷

Field task workflow

Assign anomalies to technicians, track resolution progress, and export per-technician work orders with coordinates, temperatures and recommended actions.

⛰

Topography built in

DSM/DTM rendering with hillshade, elevation probe, automatic contour generation at any interval, and elevation profiles — contours export straight to CAD.

👁

Client view

One click turns the workspace into a polished read-only portal: anomalies open as cards with photos, temperatures, severity and the recommended action.

📷

Field imagery, auto-linked

Bulk-upload inspection photos — GPS and timestamps read from EXIF, images linked to anomalies by field name or proximity, shown on the map and in reports.

🔒

Private by design

Everything runs locally in the browser. Orthos, anomalies and reports never leave your machine — projects save to a single portable file.

Workflow

From orthomosaic to client-ready report in four steps

Upload orthos

Thermal & RGB GeoTIFFs load with their CRS read automatically. DSMs render as hillshaded terrain.

Auto-detect & classify

One click scans the thermal grid — anomalies land pre-classified with T max, ΔT, severity and module IDs, flagged for QC review.

Review & enrich

Verify against the visual layer, link field photos, adjust classes in the spreadsheet grid, assign technicians.

Deliver

Technical PDF, executive summary, interactive HTML report, CAD drawings, work orders — or hand over the live portal itself.

GIS interoperability

Two-way live sync with QGIS. No server required.

Your GIS team keeps working in QGIS; your client keeps the portal. A shared sync file and our QGIS plugin move edits both ways within seconds — geometry, classes, temperatures, statuses and plant IDs intact. Or round-trip manually via GeoJSON with preserved feature IDs.

1Connect a sync file in TerraView Pro (Chrome/Edge)
2Open the same file in QGIS with the TerraView Sync plugin
3Layers load styled by class — edit anywhere
4Changes reflect on the other side within ~2 seconds
