Our Terrestrial Scanning Service

Laser scanning provides rapid acquisition of accurate 3D data in a time and cost-effective manner. Sky TerraEye has the technical and practical expertise to utilise the latest technology, giving clients unmatched, cost-effective and high-accuracy survey work for civil, construction and industrial projects across the UAE.

Our laser scanning technology captures 3D point clouds using mobile mapping systems and terrestrial laser scanners. We undertake BIM and construction projects with resources to create 3D models in any format or Level of Detail (LOD 100-500). Our team uses the latest software and customised automated extraction methods for civil, construction and infrastructure sectors.

What We Offer

Terrestrial Scanning Sub-Services

Every project is different. Here are all the specific terrestrial scanning services we provide across Dubai and the UAE.

3D Laser Scanning

High-density 3D point cloud capture of buildings, structures, bridges and industrial facilities using terrestrial laser scanners.

3D Modelling & Structures

Creation of accurate 3D models from point cloud data for structural analysis, clash detection and renovation design.

Mobile Mapper

Mobile laser mapping of roads, tunnels and large outdoor sites at vehicle speeds for rapid coverage.

BIM — Building Information Modelling

Revit, IFC and BIM model creation from scan data to any Level of Detail for AEC and construction projects.

Point Cloud Processing

Registration, classification and delivery in LAS, LAZ, E57, RCP and all standard point cloud formats.

As-Built 3D Documentation

Comprehensive as-built documentation combining 3D scans, photographs and CAD drawings for project records.

Industrial Plant Scanning

3D documentation of oil and gas facilities, processing plants and industrial assets for maintenance and modification.

Heritage Documentation

Precise 3D documentation of heritage buildings and monuments for preservation, restoration and digital archiving.

Facade & Structural Surveys

Detailed scan surveys of building facades and structural elements for condition assessment and renovation.
