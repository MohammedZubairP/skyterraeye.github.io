Start the local agent

Download the agent files and run them on your workstation. The browser will connect to it at localhost:7842. Your images never leave your machine.

# First time — download from the site, then:
conda activate ortho
python agent.py

# Or just double-click:
launch.bat (Windows)
./launch.sh (Mac / Linux)
