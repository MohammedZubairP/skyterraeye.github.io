Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a land survey take in Dubai?

Most small to medium sites can be surveyed within 1–2 days depending on size and access.

What is the difference between total station and GPS survey?

Total station provides high-precision local measurements, while GNSS/GPS supports control and coordinate validation.

Do you provide DWG files?

Yes, professional survey deliverables should include DWG and PDF formats for consultant submission.
