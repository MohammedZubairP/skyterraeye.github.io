Total Station Survey in Dubai

Total station surveying is one of the most critical services for construction projects in Dubai. It ensures accurate layout, structural alignment, and verification before and during execution.

Where Total Station is Used

  • Column and grid setting out
  • Road and infrastructure alignment
  • Foundation layout
  • Steel structure positioning
  • As-built verification

Why Accuracy Matters in Dubai

High-rise construction and infrastructure projects in Dubai require millimeter-level precision. Incorrect layout can result in delays, rework, and consultant rejection.

Deliverables

  • DWG layout drawings
  • Coordinate lists
  • Verification reports
  • QA/QC documentation
