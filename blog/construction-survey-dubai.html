A construction survey in Dubai is any surveying service that supports the design, construction or documentation of a building, road, infrastructure or development project. Construction surveys span the full project lifecycle — from initial topographic surveys and GNSS control networks before construction begins, through to final as-built documentation for Dubai Municipality building completion.

Types of Construction Surveys in Dubai

Sky TerraEye provides all types of construction survey support including:

1. Pre-Construction Surveys

  • Topographic Survey — existing ground levels, contours and features for design
  • Utility Mapping — underground services detection before excavation
  • Dilapidation Survey — condition record of adjacent structures before works begin
  • Control Network — GNSS control points to guide all subsequent surveys

2. Construction Phase Surveys

  • Setting Out — marking foundations, columns, grids and alignments on site
  • Level Control — benchmarks, floor levels, road levels and earthworks checks
  • Progress Surveys — regular verification of construction against design
  • QA/QC Surveys — checking structural elements against tolerance requirements

3. Post-Construction Surveys

  • As-Built Survey — documenting the completed construction for DM completion certificate
  • Floor Level Survey — final floor levels for handover certificate
  • Infrastructure As-Built — roads, drainage and utilities documented for authority adoption
  • Settlement Monitoring — ongoing monitoring of completed structures

Dubai Municipality Requirements for Construction Surveys

Dubai Municipality requires construction surveys to comply with DM Spatial Data standards at all stages. Key requirements include:

  • Coordinate System: WGS84 / UTM Zone 40N / EGM2008
  • Setting Out — must be tied to DM-registered control points
  • As-Built Surveys — required for building completion certificate, formatted to DM standards
  • Survey Stamp — all submitted drawings must carry the licensed surveyor’s stamp
