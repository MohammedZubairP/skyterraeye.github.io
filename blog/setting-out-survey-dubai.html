Setting Out Survey in Dubai — Construction Layout Guide (2025)

Setting Out Survey Dubai

A setting out survey in Dubai (also called a construction layout survey) is the process of transferring coordinates and dimensions from design drawings onto the ground so that construction can be built in the correct position. It is one of the most critical surveying activities on any construction project in the UAE.

What is a Setting Out Survey?

Setting out — sometimes called staking out or layout survey — is the process of using a total station or GNSS RTK rover to mark the positions of structural elements including columns, pile caps, retaining walls, foundations, road alignments and utility trenches on the ground, based on approved engineering drawings.

In Dubai, setting out surveys are required at every stage of construction, from initial foundation setting out through to final level checks for floor slabs, road kerbs and external works.

Types of Setting Out Surveys in Dubai

  • Foundation Setting Out — Marking pile cap positions, pad footing locations and pile positions from structural drawings
  • Column Grid Setting Out — Establishing the primary structural grid on each floor of a building
  • Wall Setting Out — Marking internal and external wall positions for masonry and concrete walls
  • Road Alignment Setting Out — Staking horizontal and vertical alignment for new road construction
  • Kerb Line Setting Out — Marking kerb positions for road and car park construction
  • Drainage and Utility Setting Out — Marking pipeline routes, manhole positions and utility trench alignments
  • Level Control — Establishing benchmarks and checking floor levels, slab levels and road levels against design

Why Accurate Setting Out Matters in Dubai

Inaccurate setting out in Dubai can have serious and costly consequences for a construction project:

  • Column misalignment can require structural redesign or demolition
  • Foundation position errors can cause DM rejection and delays to building permits
  • Road alignment errors can result in costly re-excavation and material waste
  • Level errors can cause drainage problems, slab re-pours and authority rejections

Setting Out Process at Sky TerraEye in Dubai

  1. Receive Drawings: We study your approved structural, architectural or civil drawings and prepare a setting out plan
  2. Establish Site Control: We set up or verify site control points tied to the DM coordinate system
  3. Total Station Setup: The total station is set over a control point, oriented, and backsight checks are performed
  4. Setting Out: All required points are set out and marked with nails, paint or stakes as required
  5. Verification: An independent check is performed on all set out points before handover
  6. Setting Out Report: A PDF report listing all set out coordinates is issued for your records
