Setting Out Survey in Dubai

Setting out survey transfers design coordinates from drawings to the physical site. It ensures the structure is built exactly as per approved plans.

Common Setting Out Works

  • Building grid layout
  • Column marking
  • Road centerline layout
  • Utility alignment
  • Foundation marking
