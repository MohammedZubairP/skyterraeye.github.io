What Affects Survey Cost in Dubai?

  • Site area — larger plots take longer to survey and cost more
  • Survey type — as-built and setting out are simpler than full topographic or GNSS control surveys
  • Number of floors — multi-storey buildings require more time on site than single-storey
  • Access conditions — restricted access, working at height or hazardous environments add time
  • Location — sites outside Dubai may have mobilisation charges depending on distance
  • Urgency — same-day mobilisation requests may carry a small premium depending on schedule

How to Get the Best Value Survey in Dubai

  • Book early — give your surveyor 24–48 hours notice for standard projects
  • Share drawings upfront — send your floor plan or DM plot number when requesting a quote — this speeds up pricing and avoids surprises
  • Bundle surveys — if you need setting out AND as-built, booking both together is usually cheaper than separate visits
  • Consider a monthly retainer — for ongoing construction projects, a retainer from AED 6,500/month saves 30–40% vs per-visit pricing
  • Get a fixed quote — never agree to hourly rates without a cap. Sky TerraEye always quotes a fixed price
