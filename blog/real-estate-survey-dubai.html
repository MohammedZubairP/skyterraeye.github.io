Why Get a Survey Before Buying Property in Dubai?

  • Boundary discrepancies — the physical boundary may differ from the DLD title deed
  • Encroachments — neighbouring structures built over your boundary
  • Municipality reserves — DM setback strips that reduce your buildable area
  • Actual plot area — confirming the plot area matches your title deed
  • Building deviations — structures not matching approved drawings (affects future DLD registration)
  • Unauthorised additions — rooms, extensions or buildings added without permits

Types of Real Estate Surveys in Dubai

Boundary Survey (Plot Demarcation)

Boundary survey physically locates and marks your legal plot corners using DLD registered coordinates. The most important survey before any land purchase in Dubai.

As-Built Verification

As-built survey documents existing structures confirming they match approved DM building permit drawings. Critical for properties where unauthorised additions may exist.

Combined Pre-Purchase Survey

Boundary confirmation + existing building as-built in one site visit — the recommended package before finalising any Dubai property purchase.

How to Confirm a Plot Boundary in Dubai

  1. Obtain your DLD title deed with registered boundary coordinates
  2. Sky TerraEye plots the DLD coordinates on site and confirms where your legal boundary falls
  3. Boundary corners marked with iron pins or concrete markers
  4. DM-compliant boundary drawing produced with dimensions, area and coordinate schedule
  5. Any discrepancies between physical and legal boundaries are reported in writing
