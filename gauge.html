Gauge is a free precision measurement utility by Sky TerraEye. All calculations use WGS84 ellipsoid, Haversine formula for geodesic distances, Shoelace theorem for polygon area, and Bowring Transverse Mercator series for UTM projection. Results are for reference and planning purposes only — not for legal boundary delimitation or official survey submission without field verification.
