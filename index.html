FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about land surveying in Dubai.

How much does a land survey cost in Dubai?
Survey costs depend on site area, type and scope. A residential setting-out or as-built survey starts from AED 1,500. Commercial projects are quoted individually. Contact us for a free quote within 2 hours.
Are your surveys accepted by Dubai Municipality?
Yes. All our surveys are delivered in WGS84 / UTM Zone 40N / EGM2008 as required by Dubai Municipality Spatial Data standards. Our DM submission pass rate is 99% on first attempt.
How long does a topographic survey take?
A standard residential plot takes 1-2 field days with CAD deliverables within 24-48 hours. Setting-out and as-built surveys can often be completed same-day.
Do you cover areas outside Dubai?
Yes. Sky TerraEye covers all seven Emirates including Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman and Ras Al Khaimah. Mobilisation charges may apply outside Dubai.
What file formats do you deliver?
AutoCAD DWG, PDF A1/A3, CSV/Excel coordinate lists and LandXML. Revit, Civil 3D and BIM-compatible formats on request.
