Utility Detection & Mapping
Utility Mapping

Underground Utility Mapping & GPR Surveys

Utility surveying is the process of identifying and labelling public utility mains that are underground. This accounts for telecommunication lines, electricity distribution, natural gas, cable television, fiber optics, traffic lights, street lights, storm drains, water mains, and waste water pipes. We use state-of-the-art underground cable locators and GPR (Ground Penetrating Radar) to efficiently detect and trace underground utilities before any subsurface work is carried out.

  • Mitigating potential conflicts in design to avoid costly delays
  • Conducting conflict analysis of utilities clashing with design
  • Validating existing utility locations and data from asset owner plans
  • Searching for unknown utilities using a range of geophysical techniques

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from contractors and developers about land surveying in Dubai.

How much does a land survey cost in Dubai?
Survey costs depend on site area, type and scope. A standard residential setting-out or as-built survey starts from AED 1,500. Commercial and infrastructure projects are quoted individually. Contact us for a free quote within 2 hours.
Are your surveys accepted by Dubai Municipality?
Yes. All our surveys are delivered in WGS84 coordinates, UTM Zone 40N projection and EGM2008 height datum as required by Dubai Municipality Spatial Data standards. Our DM submission pass rate is 99% on first attempt.
How long does a topographic survey take in Dubai?
A standard topographic survey for a residential plot typically takes 1–2 field days, with CAD deliverables ready within 24–48 hours. Larger sites are assessed individually.
Do you do setting-out surveys for construction in Dubai?
Yes. Sky TerraEye provides full construction setting-out services in Dubai including column grids, foundation layouts, floor levels and road alignments. We work with contractors, developers and consultants across the UAE.
Do you cover areas outside Dubai?
Yes. Sky TerraEye covers all seven Emirates including Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah and Fujairah. Mobilisation charges may apply for sites outside Dubai.
What file formats do you deliver?
Standard deliverables include AutoCAD DWG (2018 compatible), PDF A1/A3, CSV/Excel coordinate lists and LandXML. Revit, Civil 3D and BIM-compatible formats are available on request.
