Workflow

Capture → Process → Map → Analyze → Deliver

  1. Plan: flight plans, control network, safety & permits
  2. Capture: UAV RGB/LiDAR/Thermal + GNSS/TS checks
  3. Process: photogrammetry/LiDAR pipelines with QA
  4. Map: orthos, DTM/DSM, contours & breaklines
  5. Analyze: volumes, plan vs actual, defect tickets
  6. Deliver: DWG/DXF/SHP, PDFs, dashboards & data rooms
