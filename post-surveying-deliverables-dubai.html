Survey deliverables checklist (DWG + PDF)

A checklist we use internally to keep outputs clean, consistent, and approval-friendly.

What a consultant‑ready deliverables pack includes

  • DWG with clean layers, blocks, and naming
  • Coordinate table (CSV/PDF) with point IDs, N/E/Z, description
  • Level report (BM, HI, BS/FS, RLs) when leveling is in scope
  • As‑built markups and deviation notes (if any)

DWG structure

  • Separate layers for existing, proposed, control, text, dimensions
  • North arrow, scale, legend, and coordinate system note
  • Title block with revision, dates, and survey method
