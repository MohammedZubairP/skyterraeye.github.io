📚 About This Converter

This tool converts WGS84 geographic coordinates (latitude/longitude) to UTM Zone 40N grid coordinates (Easting/Northing) as required by Dubai Municipality Spatial Data standards for all land survey submissions.

Dubai falls entirely within UTM Zone 40N. All DM submissions must use WGS84 datum with UTM Zone 40N projection. Heights must be in EGM2008 orthometric datum — not GPS ellipsoidal heights.
