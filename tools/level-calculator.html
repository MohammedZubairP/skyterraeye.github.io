🎓 How to Use This Calculator

Enter BS (Backsight) — the first reading taken to a known benchmark or turning point. Enter FS (Foresight) — the last reading before moving the instrument. Enter IS (Intermediate Sight) for intermediate points. The calculator automatically computes Rise & Fall and Reduced Levels in EGM2008 orthometric heights as required by Dubai Municipality. Closure error should be less than ±12√K mm (where K = distance in km) for Class C surveys accepted by DM.
