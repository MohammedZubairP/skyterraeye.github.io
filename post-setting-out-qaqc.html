Setting‑out QA/QC on construction sites

Simple routines that reduce rework and improve consultant confidence.

Before you set‑out: control validation

  • Confirm benchmark/source control and coordinate basis
  • Run redundant checks on at least two known points
  • Record instrument height and prism height clearly

On‑site routine for Total Station work

  • Backsight orientation check (repeat)
  • Re-section check if control shifts
  • Independent check shot on a critical point

After set‑out: documentation

  • Photo evidence of markings where required
  • Coordinate table and stakeout report
  • Deviation notes if site conditions force changes
