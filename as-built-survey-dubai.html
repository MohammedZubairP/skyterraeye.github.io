As-Built Survey & CAD Drawings in Dubai

As-built surveys in Dubai with professional CAD drawings: capture final positions, levels and deviations, then deliver DWG/PDF reports for consultant submissions.

Scope

  • Measure completed works to capture true installed position
  • Compare against design to identify deviations and tolerances
  • Provide coordinate lists, levels, and key dimensions
  • Produce clean CAD drawings with layers and labels
  • Support consultant/main-contractor submission packs

FAQ

What is included in an as-built survey report?
Usually: measured coordinates/levels, deviation comparison, drawings (DWG/PDF), and supporting notes for consultant review.
Can you align layers with our CAD standard?
Yes. Share your CAD layering standard and title block requirements.
Can you do as-built for utilities or trenches?
Yes, if the scope is measurable and access is available; we document what’s visible and captured.
